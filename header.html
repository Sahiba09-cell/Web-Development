Facts

Primarily focused on developing water resources and generating hydroelectric power. Key Projects: Manages major hydroelectric projects, including the Tarbela and Mangla dams. History & Structure: Founded in 1958, it was restructured in 2007, dividing its responsibilities. While WAPDA handles hydropower, the Pakistan Electric Power Company (PEPCO) handles thermal power, distribution, and billing. Role in Power Sector: It is the largest hydropower supplier in Pakistan, providing over 95% of the country's hydropower capacity as of 2019. Headquarters: Based in Lahore.
